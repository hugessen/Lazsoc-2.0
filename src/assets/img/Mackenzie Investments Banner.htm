Laurier Sales Association added an event.
March 20

This Thursday, March 23rd, Laurier Sales Association is hosting its FINAL event of the 2016/2017 year. We are welcoming back Mackenzie Investments to campus! This company is one of the top financial service organizations in the world, and once again we are bringing in a top executive, Regional Vice President, Mike Evans! This event is focused around creating a "mental imprint" to have in your pocket for future interactions. Join us in learning how to turn your unique brand into a conversation. Open to everyone and especially encouraged to all finance/accounting students who may not see where sales fits into these industries (trust us, it does!). Dinner will be provided. Do not miss out on your chance to network and gain valuable insight from an industry leader!

Looking forward to seeing everyone there, business casual please!
